We'd Like To Teach You Far Away

Teaching someone to dive can be challenging, as the students can injure themself if they haven't mastered the proper form. Demonstrating the diving stance on land and showing them a kneeling dive can help a swimmer get comfortable with diving into the water headfirst. You can then move on to teaching them a standing dive so they learn the correct form. Instructors always have to stay close to the swimmer as students learn how to dive so they can assist them as needed.
